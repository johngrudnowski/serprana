Meet Kate, Founder of SERPRANA

My path in holistic wellness began in 2015, guided by deep intuition, a strong connection to Nature, and a devotion to living from the heart.

My studies in permaculture and herbalism in Guatemala, and time with the Kogi and Wiwa tribes of the Sierra Nevada, keepers of ancient Earth wisdom, cultivated a deep connection to Nature that guides how I listen, connect, and partner with the Earth. My path with the Universidad de la Alquimia expanded my connection to alchemy and Archangelic energies.

As a Holistic Alchemist, I harmonize the energetic, emotional, physical, and mental bodies through sound, intuition, and plant wisdom, transmuting density and guiding you back into clarity, alignment, confidence, and embodied self-worth.

Over the past four years, I’ve shared sound medicine and plant wisdom at retreats and festivals across Costa Rica and Panama, including Radio Venao, Reactor, and Yoga Gathering Venao, and continue expanding globally.
