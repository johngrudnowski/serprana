Meet Kate

My path in holistic wellness began in 2015 — guided by intuition, a deep connection to Nature, and a devotion to living from the heart.

I’ve been shaped by time with the Kogi and Wiwa tribes of the Sierra Nevada — keepers of one of the oldest unbroken traditions of Earth wisdom on the planet — and by the teachers at the Universidad de la Alquimia.

As a Holistic Alchemist I combine sound, energy, intuition, and plant wisdom to support the transformation of your energy, emotions, and patterns — shifting heaviness into clarity, alignment, and empowerment.
